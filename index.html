Everything We Handle — So You Don't Have To

A Fleet Built for BC's Roads

Our modern moving trucks are fully equipped with furniture pads, heavy-duty straps, loading ramps, and tie-downs — everything needed to protect your belongings on every road, from city streets to mountain highways.

Local Moves & Long-Distance Hauls

Relocating within the Lower Mainland or heading to the BC Interior, Vancouver Island, or Alberta? We coordinate every detail — dedicated drivers, full cargo insurance, and a confirmed delivery window you can actually rely on.

White-Glove Packing, Every Time

Fragile antiques, flat-screen TVs, pianos, fine art — nothing leaves your home without being double-wrapped, padded, and properly secured. Our trained crew treats every item as if it were irreplaceable, because to you, it is.

Containerized Storage, Delivered to Your Door

Between moves, renovating, or downsizing? Our weatherproof containers are brought right to your driveway, loaded at your pace, then transported to our secure Port Coquitlam facility. Access your belongings on your schedule — no extra handling, no damage risk.

Our Port Coquitlam Warehouse

Fully fenced, camera-monitored 24/7, and staffed during business hours — our storage facility is built to keep your belongings safe and dry. Short-term between closes or long-term through a life transition, we have the space and the security to handle it.

100 Containers, Perfectly Organized

Each container is individually locked and labeled. Your items stay separate, protected, and easy to access — no rummaging through a shared unit.

Moving Supplies — Pick Up or Pre-Order

Stop by our Port Coquitlam supply store for boxes in every size, heavy-duty tape, bubble wrap, mattress bags, dish kits, and specialty packing materials. Or simply tell us what you need and we'll bring it all on moving day — nothing left to chance.

Red-Carpet Protection — Included Standard

We lay neoprene carpet runners throughout your home and install door-frame guards before a single box is moved. When we leave, your floors, walls, and paint are in exactly the same condition as when we arrived — guaranteed.
