Everything you need to know about booking, pricing, and what to expect on moving day.

Ask us anything →
One of our estimators visits your home, walks through every room, and takes inventory of what needs to move. You'll receive a written all-in price — covering labour, truck, materials, and any stairs or long carries — before we book anything. No pressure, no obligation, no surprises on bill day.
Yes — we're open Monday through Sunday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Weekend moves book fast, especially on month-end dates, so we recommend requesting your estimate at least 2–3 weeks ahead when possible.
We cover the entire Lower Mainland — Port Coquitlam, Coquitlam, Burnaby, Vancouver, Surrey, New Westminster, Langley, and beyond. We also handle long-distance moves to the BC Interior, Vancouver Island, and Alberta.
Yes. OnTime Moving is fully licensed by the BC Passenger Transportation Board and carries comprehensive cargo and liability insurance. We're also BBB A+ accredited. All crew members are background-checked and uniformed.
Every move includes standard cargo insurance. If anything is damaged during transport, we file a claim immediately and resolve it — no runaround, no fine print loopholes. We also use floor runners, door-frame guards, and double-wrapped padding on every job to prevent damage from happening in the first place.
For peak months (May–September) and month-end dates, 3–4 weeks is ideal. Off-peak moves can often be booked with 1–2 weeks' notice. We recommend reaching out as soon as you have a rough date — an estimate costs nothing and locks nothing in.
