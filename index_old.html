It's Easy Working With Us

1

Make a Request

Fill in the estimate form, email us directly at info@ontime-moving.com, or simply call (604) 505-0026. Once received, we'll set up an appointment to view your home.

2

Get Your Estimate

We perform an in-person estimate at your home to fully understand your needs. You'll receive a follow-up email with a detailed checklist and full breakdown of service charges.

3

Book Your Move

Once you approve the plan, pay a deposit to secure your spot. We'll send a reminder prior to service and bring all necessary supplies to ensure a smooth move.

4

Get Settled

Your move is complete! Settle the invoice with check, credit card, or e-transfer. Need storage? We'll containerize everything at your door, saving time and money.

We Make a Difference

We aim at serving families who care for quality, service and reliability. Serving over 11,000 families across the Lower Mainland for 22+ years.

Truck

Complete Moving

Door-to-door local moving across Lower Mainland, as well as long distance relocation to BC Interior or surrounding islands. Fully trained crew, highly organized and efficient.

Customized Packing

Complete or partial packing assistance to protect your belongings. We offer customized crates or containers for fragile items and provide red-carpet service.

Containerized Storage

Ideal alternative to self-storage and Steelbox. We containerize at your door, saving 30% on handling costs. 8,000 sq ft secure warehouse with 100 containers available.

Supply

One-stop shopping for all moving supplies. Boxes, tape, bubble wrap, and everything you need for self-packing to make your move smooth and secure.
