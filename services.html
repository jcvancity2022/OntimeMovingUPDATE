Moving & Storage Services

Every service you book includes a written estimate, red-carpet floor protection, and a crew that shows up on time — or we make it right.

🏠

Residential Moving

Studio apartment, condo, townhouse, or a 5-bedroom family home — our residential crews handle every scale of move with the same meticulous care. We pack, wrap, load, transport, unload, and place every item exactly where you want it. Arrive at your new home ready to live in it, not work in it.

  • Full packing & unpacking
  • Furniture disassembly & reassembly
  • Fragile & high-value item care
🏢

Commercial Relocation

Business downtime costs money. We schedule around you — after hours, weekends, or overnight — and assign a dedicated project manager who coordinates your entire move from first walkthrough to final setup. Your team walks into a fully operational office, not a pile of boxes.

  • After-hours & weekend scheduling
  • IT & server equipment handling
  • Dedicated project manager
📦

Secure Storage

We bring the container to your door, you load at your own pace, then we transport it to our fully fenced, camera-monitored Port Coquitlam facility. With 100 individual units, short-term or long-term storage is always available — and your items are never mixed with anyone else's.

  • 100 on-site containers
  • 24/7 monitored security
  • Month-to-month or long-term
🚛

Long-Distance Moving

Moving to Kelowna, Calgary, Victoria, or beyond? Our long-distance crews are experienced on BC's mountain routes and inter-provincial highways. Your shipment travels with real-time GPS tracking, comprehensive cargo insurance, and a firm delivery window — not a vague estimate that leaves you waiting by the door.

  • Real-time GPS tracking
  • Full cargo insurance
  • Guaranteed delivery dates
